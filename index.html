Computer History

Instructions

The pictures below tell a story about important events in computer history. However, the story is hidden. To find the story, you must have the soul of a hacker. Here are some clues.

  • Use your Google search skills to find the name of the computer or person in each picture. Scan the search results to gather a little additional information as well.
  • Then use the information you gathered to guess the name of a hidden page on this website. Type the page name you guessed into the address box. If you're right, you'll find one chapter of the story. Hint! Don't use capital letters in page names.
  • When you find the page, complete the printed Computer History worksheet.
