Computer History

Instructions

The pictures below tell a story about important events in the history of computers. However, the story is hidden. To find the story, you must have the soul of a hacker.

  • Use your Google search skills to find the name of the computer or person in each picture. Sometimes it's helpful to read the search results to gather a little additional information as well.
  • Use the name or information to guess the name of a hidden page on this website. Type the page name you guessed into the address box. If you're right, you'll find one chapter of the story.
  • Don't use capital letters in page names.
